My Favorite Pop Divas – Part 1

These are the women who inspire me not just with their music but with everything else. (No ranked order I love them too much)

Beyonce
I chose her because of how she exudes confidence and I have not been the most confident so I try to emulate her.
Lady Gaga
All gay people love her, and as a weird kid she made me feel seen.
Kesha
She was my introduction into the club scene which has changed my life so I thank her.
Adele
So I was a chubby kid, so seeing someone plus size like her be so talented and amazing will always have her in my heart.
Rihanna
Her music is so just varied and amazing I love how she destroys genre tropes and does her own thing (and shes a tame impala fan so shes super cool in my book).
Britney Spears
I love her dearly and I wish the world was kinder to her, as a young 20 something I could only image the stress she was under.
