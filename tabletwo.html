My Favorite Pop Divas – Part 2

This page is for the newer pop singers that I find lovely and I cannot wait to see what they do as time goes on.

Charli xcx
She made the summer of 2024 the best, and I haven’t really felt like myself since then so I love her for what she did for me.
Sophie Xeon
Her music I swear changed my brain chemistry, her death is a very sore subject for me but I love her dearly.
Lisa
I knew I needed someone from KPOP here, and I love Blackpink so I wanted to include my favorite from the group.
Chappell Roan
We are both gay and from the middle of Missouri who made it out, she inspires me and gives me hope my situation can get better no matter what.
Pinkpantheress
She is the new hot thing, and I am so happy to be along for the ride. She makes me realize the British aren’t all bad.
Zara Larsson
Her song Midnight Sun alone got her on this list, but I also enjoy her activism.
